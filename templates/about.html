Our Story

Founded in 2025, moto-x was born from a universal experience: that sinking feeling of losing something valuable. What started as a simple idea to solve this problem has grown into a passionate team dedicated to building a bridge between the lost and the found. Our journey is driven by the belief that every lost item has a story, and we want to help write its happy ending.

Our Mission

To reunite people with their cherished belongings. We provide a simple, powerful platform that connects owners with the kind people who find their items, bringing peace of mind back to our community.
